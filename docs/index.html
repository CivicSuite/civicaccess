CivicSuite / CivicAccess

Accessibility support that keeps humans responsible.

CivicAccess is the accessibility, plain-language, multilingual, and ADA Title II review-support module for CivicSuite.

v1.0.0 public-use support release

Current State

The package ships deterministic accessibility review support, optional database-backed review records via CIVICACCESS_REVIEW_DB_URL, accessible form planning, accessible publishing workflow checks, plain-language rewrite, multilingual draft variants, ADA Title II review-support packages, tagged-PDF expectations, records-ready export checklists, and a functional public UI.

CivicAccess does not provide legal advice, certified ADA compliance, official translation certification, live LLM calls, or final publication approval.

Product Promise

  • Surface actionable accessibility fixes with WCAG references.
  • Rewrite municipal jargon into plainer public language.
  • Mark multilingual variants for human review before publication.
  • Support ADA Title II review workflows without claiming certification.
  • Preserve source/rewrite provenance for records requests.
